# Product Requirements Document: Luxury Jet Recommendation Web App

## Core Concept

A premium, mobile-first web application designed for a private jet operator. It serves as an interactive concierge, pre-qualifying potential buyers by guiding them through an elegant, seamless questionnaire. Based on their inputs, the system intelligently recommends the most suitable aircraft from the predefined fleet, presenting a primary match and alternatives. The experience emphasizes a "Dark Luxury" aesthetic with cinematic transitions, minimal design, and glassmorphism elements, culminating in a structured lead capture.

## 10-Question Structure

1. **Primary Usage:** What is the primary purpose of your travel? (Business, Leisure, Mixed)
2. **Passenger Count:** How many passengers typically travel with you?
3. **Typical Destinations:** What are your most frequent flight routes? (e.g., NY to LA, London to Dubai)
4. **Core Priority:** What is your top priority when flying? (Speed, Comfort, Range, Cost-Efficiency, Privacy)
5. **Luggage / Cargo:** What are your usual luggage requirements? (Light, Standard, Heavy/Oversized)
6. **Cabin Preferences:** Do you require specific cabin features? (e.g., Master suite, Conference table, Shower)
7. **Base Location:** Where will the aircraft primarily be based?
8. **Flight Hours:** What is your estimated annual flight volume? (Hours per year)
9. **Budget Range:** What is your anticipated budget for acquisition?
10. **Contact Details:** Where should we send your personalized dossier? (Name, Email, Phone)
